About This Project

This is a digital voting platform built for Burnside High School's prefect elections, replacing the previous Google Forms-based process. It was designed and built by Mahi Mahatab, a Burnside student, as an NCEA Digital Technologies project, developed with the support of Mr. Rodkiss.

The system authenticates voters through their school Google account, prevents duplicate voting at the database level, and separates "who voted" from "who they voted for" everywhere in the system, including for administrators.

Source code: Astral-Viscount/Prefect-Voting
