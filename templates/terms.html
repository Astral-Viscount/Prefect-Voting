Last updated: 18 July 2026 — applies to the Burnside High School Prefect Voting system.

1. Purpose

This system exists to run Burnside High School's prefect elections. By logging in, you agree to use it only for that purpose.

2. Eligibility

You may access this system if you hold a valid Burnside High School Google account (ending in @burnside.school.nz), or have been explicitly granted access as an election administrator.

3. One Account, One Vote

Each position can be voted on once per account. This is enforced by the system itself, not just by these terms — a second vote for a position you've already voted in will always be rejected.

4. Prohibited Conduct

  • Attempting to vote more than once for the same position, including by any technical means
  • Sharing your login credentials with another student, or voting on behalf of someone else
  • Impersonating another student or staff member
  • Attempting to access administrator functionality without authorisation
  • Attempting to interfere with, overload, or disrupt the system
  • Offering or accepting any incentive in exchange for a vote

5. Candidate Content

Candidates are responsible for their own profile content (bio, photo, voice introduction). Content must follow the school's usual standard of student conduct. Administrators may remove inappropriate content without notice.

6. Consequences of Breach

Suspected breaches of these terms will be referred to the relevant Dean or Deputy Principal under the school's usual disciplinary process. Votes found to have been cast fraudulently may be voided at the election administrators' discretion.

7. Availability

Administrators will make reasonable efforts to keep the system available throughout the voting window, but cannot guarantee uninterrupted access. If a significant outage occurs during voting, administrators may extend the voting window to compensate.

8. Branding

9. Changes

These terms may be updated between election cycles. Continued use of the system after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
