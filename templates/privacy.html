Privacy Policy

This is highkey ai written yo. This is just a temporary placeholder for proper privacy policies.

Last updated: 18 July 2026. Written in plain language — if anything here is unclear, contact us using the details at the bottom.

What we collect

  • From Google Sign-In: your name, school email address, and a unique Google account ID
  • If you're a candidate: the bio, photo, and optional voice introduction you choose to add
  • For every voter: which positions you have voted in, and when — but never which candidate you chose

What we deliberately do not do

Your vote choice is never stored anywhere that links it back to you in a way any admin, report, or export can see. The "who has voted" list administrators can view only ever shows that you voted, never for whom. The administrative audit log only ever records actions administrators take (creating an election, promoting an admin, exporting a report) — it is never able to record a vote choice, because the code that writes to it is never called from the voting process at all.

Who can see what

  • Voters see their own account and published results
  • Candidates see their own profile editor, and their own result once the election has closed (never live, so it can't affect campaigning)
  • Administrators see turnout figures, aggregate results, and the list of who has voted — never individual vote choices

Third parties

The only third party involved is Google, used solely to verify your identity when you sign in. This system does not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics, and does not sell or share your data with anyone outside the school.

Cookies & local storage

We use one session cookie to keep you signed in, and a security token to protect forms from forgery. If you use dark mode, your preference is remembered in your browser's local storage. None of this is used for tracking or advertising.

Data retention

Data is kept for the duration of the election and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping, then archived or deleted in line with the school's usual records practice.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you can ask what information we hold about you and request it be corrected. Contact us using the details below.
